Gláuber Barbacovi Ribeiro

Who is Gláuber?

Gláuber is the eldest of 3 brothers (Rosceli and Levy), son of Odair and Ariela Ribeiro, born on April 3, 2004 in Campinas do Sul, the city in which he currently resides. In addition to being a multi-instrumentalist musician, he is a Computer Science student and developer with an affinity for Front-end.

Academic Training

He studied at Nossa Senhora da Paz and Geny Telles Colpani Schools in elementary school, participating and winning Bronze medal at the OBA e OBMEP Olympics.
Graduated IT Maintenance and Support Technician, Integrated High School Course at the Federal Institute of Rio Grande do Sul from which he also obtained another Bronze medal in OBA Olympic.
Currently studying the 5th Semester of Computer Science at the Integrated Regional University of Alto Uruguai and Missões.
